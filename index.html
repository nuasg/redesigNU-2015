Submit you're best designs and applications to improve Northwestern for a chance to win cash prizes!

    Use released APIs like Sodexo and CAESAR.

  • Network

    Meet new people through your teams, or link up with friends for a killer app.

  • Win

    Showcase your skill set in front of a distinguished panel of judges, the Northwestern community, and win great prizes

Who can participate?

You! Anyone currently enrolled in Northwestern, graduate and undergraduate, is able to register. We recommend, but is not required, that teams be made up of program managers, graphic designers, and programmers.

Can I register as a team?

Absolutely! Build an app on your own or with a team. Just include your team members' names on the registration page. And don't forget that each team member must submit a separate registration!

I don't have a team, what about me?

No worries - there will be an opportunity to create teams at the orientation.

I don't code, what about me?

You've found the perfect place and time to learn! We'll release guides for everyone as well as the opportunity to join a team of coders!

Don't want to use any of the data at the event?

That's great - all ideas that will improve the student experience, in any way, are welcomed!
